When In Rome

When Venus came to tempt my love,
some fervor I withdrew.
But my lover said, "When in Rome
do as the Romans do."

Minerva cried in vexation,
Juno I no loger knew,
Mars sough shelter in my thoughts
when Jupitor's cyclones blew.


But as I try as I did (hardly at all)
Venus will not subdue.
For the Romans said,"When in love,
do as the lovers do"



-Megan Alms-
